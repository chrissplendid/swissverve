A Brief History About Us

A few years ago, a small team of people determined to transform banking launched a savings app for everyone. That app was the first step toward Swissverve Bank. Today, we're even more determined and we've built a Central Bank-licensed, microfinance bank to help you get the best out of your money without overcharging you. Swissverver includes tools for tracking your spending habits, saving more and making the right money moves. So no matter who you are or where you live, we're here because of you. We know the pain that comes with using a regular bank and we will make things work better for everyone.
